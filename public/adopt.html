Adoption Process

Interested in adopting? Here is how the process works:

1

Apply

Fill out an adoption application form. We ask about your living situation and experience with pets.

2

Meet & Greet

Schedule a visit to meet the cat and see if you are a good match for each other.

3

Home Check

A quick review to make sure the environment is safe and suitable for the cat.

4

Welcome Home

Once approved, take your new furry friend home. We provide a starter kit and follow-up support!
